
How to export capture as .pcap in Wireshark (GUI):
1. Start Wireshark and capture on your active network interface.
2. After capturing, go to File -> Save As...
3. Choose format "pcap" or "pcapng" and pick a filename, e.g., synthetic_capture.pcap
4. Click Save. The .pcap file is ready for submission.
